Life in England

After his abduction Bennelong lived in England for two years before returning home in 1795.

England Bennelong perfomed cultural perfomances with a young Eora boy called Yemmerrawanne.

He was dressed in extremely fancy clothing as he was a living representative of the Eora tribe and Govener Phillip hoped this would end up in a formal treaty.

Bennelong enjoyed exploring the areas and visited many tourist attractions that are still renowned today such as: The Tower of London, The Houses of Parliament and St Paul's Cathedral
