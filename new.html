Why was Bennelong a Contributor to the Colonisation of Australia?

Wether or not Bennelong is hard to answer but he played a huge role in the development of connection with the European and learned their language.

Bennelong was the first Aboriginal man to travel to England and back. He taught some of the colonisors their language and was highly interactive and curious with what they were doing.

Bennelong helped teach the Europeans the ways and boundaries of the land and its inhabitants. He was also a form of communication across the two nations.

He was a helpful contributor to mapping out the land and was confident in helping Phillip and his men understand the cultural practises of his people.
